# Day 1 – CRM Basics

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
It helps companies manage customers and business workflows.

## What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.

## Account
Represents an organization or company.

## Contact
Represents a person related to the account.

## Opportunity
Represents a potential business deal.

## Real World Mapping
Account = College  
Contact = Student  
Lead = Student interested in admission  
Opportunity = Admission process
